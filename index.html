Pepin graduated from McGill University with a degree in Cultural Studies and continued her studies in Broadcast Journalism & Communications at the University of Leeds, England. In 2018, she obtained a certificate of achievement from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Tamy has collaborated with renowned brands and partners such as the London School of Economics, Oxfam, Tourism Montreal, and the United Nations.
Tamy is available for keynotes, panels, shows and speaking engagements exploring : business & management, cultural intelligence, thought leadership diversity & inclusion, content marketing, multidisciplinary collaboration, media & innovation, ethics & artificial intelligence, arts & culture and social & women’s issues.
With a talent for synthetizing across all fields, Tamy can present and engage conversations at conferences, nonprofits, festivals, universities, and across media streams. She is perfectly fluent in French and English.

Tamy is a consultant and connector with over 13 years of experience in the creative industries and in media. She has a strong ability in narrative design, adaptive strategies, conceptual frameworks, creative direction, thought leadership, culture and experiential design.

She brings fresh perspectives on topics of diversity & inclusion, wellness, business & technology, millennials, work culture, content marketing, change management, cultural translation, education, creativity & innovation, ethics, global affairs and intelligence (AI/EI\UI).

Pepin is the CEO and Director of UPPL inc - an international content marketing agency, based in Montreal. She is also the founder of ORB - a creative ecosystem and multidisciplinary space dedicated to the development of talent, stories and experiences at the intersection of art, science and wellness. An award-winning journalist, she is the recipiendary of a Gemini Award for Best TV Host of Cultural series.
